One Pan : Seared Salmon with Lemon, Vanilla Grape Tomatoes, and Green Beans Ingredients: - [ ] Salmon fillet (skin-on, descaled, patted dry) - [ ] Oil (Extra virgin olive oil/Avocado oil/Canola oil) - [ ] Salt/garlic salt - [ ] Pepper - [ ] Lemon - [ ] Vanilla extract - [ ] Grape tomatoes/cherry tomatoes - [ ] Green beans -season salmon both sides -heat oil in pan medium-high heat until shimmering -place salmon in pan in middle skin up without moving it for 4 mins -while thats happening cut and place cherry tomatoes and place in pan with remaining oiland let them bruise and sear -also put in green beans on other side of salmon in oil and season -flip salmon and cook until done, around 4-5 mins more -transfer everything to plate and add a small amount of vanilla extract to tomatoes -make sure everything is seasoned - bon appetit!
